Recomposition Rules — Non-Negotiable

◈Eat protein within 30 min of waking and within 30 min post-workout — muscle protein synthesis window is real
◈Carbs before training (rice, oats, banana) and immediately after — this is when your body partitions them to muscle, not fat
◈No carbs after 7pm except on training days — evening insulin sensitivity drops significantly
◈Sleep 7–9 hours — GH peaks in deep sleep. This is when your body actually builds the muscle you trained. This is not optional.
◈Creatine monohydrate 5g daily — the only supplement with unambiguous evidence for lean muscle gain
◈Vegetables at every meal for satiety and micronutrients — you're in a deficit, micronutrient density matters
◈Track everything for the first 4 weeks minimum — people who track calories lose 2–3x more fat than those who estimate
◈Reduce deficit in Phase 3 to TDEE −100 — you are lean by then and need fuel to reveal the final layer of definition

Weekly Check-In Protocol

◈Weigh yourself every Monday morning, fasted, after using the bathroom — same conditions every time
◈Take a front, side and back photo every 4 weeks in the same lighting — progress photos lie less than the scale
◈If weight hasn't dropped in 2 consecutive weeks, reduce calories by 100 or add one cardio session
◈If strength is dropping significantly, you are in too large a deficit — eat more protein and reduce the calorie cut slightly
